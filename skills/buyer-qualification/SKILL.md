# Buyer Qualification & Scoring Guide

## Scoring Criteria (Weighted)

Compute a total score out of 16 points using the four weighted criteria below.

| Criterion | Weight | 1 pt | 2 pt | 3 pt | 4 pt |
|-----------|--------|------|------|------|------|
| Revenue | 30% | $1M–$5M | $5M–$20M | $20M–$100M | $100M+ |
| Employee count | 15% | 10–50 | 50–200 | 200–1000 | 1000+ |
| Import history | 25% | No history (0 pt) | Some imports | — | Active importer in category |
| Product relevance | 30% | No overlap (0 pt) | Adjacent category | — | Direct match |

Apply weights: multiply each raw score (0–4) by its weight percentage, then sum for a weighted total.

## Buyer Type Classification

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Importer | Buys directly from overseas; typically higher volume |
| Distributor | Resells to retailers or businesses within their market |
| Wholesaler | Bulk buyer; price-sensitive, high volume |
| Retailer | End-seller; smaller quantities, brand-conscious |

## Minimum Qualification Thresholds

A buyer must meet **all** of the following:

- Annual revenue >= $1M (default minimum revenue threshold)
- Employee count >= 10 (default minimum employee count)
- Valid business registration
- Active presence in the target market

## Priority Tier Ranking

| Tier | Score | Outreach Strategy |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| Tier 1 | >= 12 | Immediate outreach with a personalized email |
| Tier 2 | 8–11 | Standard sequence; batch outreach |
| Tier 3 | 4–7 | Low priority; include in broad campaigns only |
| Below threshold | < 4 | Do not contact |

## Disqualification Criteria (Auto-Reject)

Disqualify a buyer automatically if any of the following apply:
- Contacted within the last 90 days.
- On the user's exclusion list.
- Sanctioned entity or country.
- Invalid or missing contact information.
